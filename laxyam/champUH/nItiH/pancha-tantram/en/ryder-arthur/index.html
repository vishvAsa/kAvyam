PANCHATANTRA

TRANSLATED FROM THE SANSKRIT BY

ARTHUR W. RYDER

THE PANCHATANTRA

Whoever learns the work by heart,

Or through the story-teller’s art

Becomes Acquainted

His Life by sad defeat—although

The King of heaven be his foe—

Is never tainted.
